Refund Policy

Eligible and Non-Refundable Items

StudentShop offers refunds only for paid notes explicitly marked as Refundable  on their listing pages. All non-refundable notes are marked Refundable . If you have already returned (refunded) a note, you may re-purchase it, but that second purchase will no longer be refundable.

Refund Timeframe and Conditions

You have 24 hours from the time of purchase to request a refund. This 24‑hour window begins when you complete the checkout. If you do not initiate a refund within 24 hours, the purchase becomes final and non-refundable. Importantly, if you download the note within that period, the refund window closes immediately. The system will prompt you to confirm before downloading.

How to Request a Refund

  • Navigate to Account > Purchases.

  • Click the menu next to the purchase you want to refund, then select  Refund. A dialog box should appear.

  • In the dialog box, pick a reason and provide a brief explanation for your request.

  • Upon submission, the refunded amount will be credited to your StudentShop balance. You may withdraw these funds to a PayPal account at any time.

Valid Refund Reasons

StudentShop accepts refunds for any of the three listed reasons: “Accidental purchase,” “Change of mind,” or “Not as described.” In other words, you can request a refund regardless of whether you simply regret the purchase, clicked the wrong item, or feel the note didn’t match its description.

Refund Amounts and Processing Fees

When a refund is processed, the full purchase price minus a portion of the PayPal transaction fee will be credited back to you. The deduction amount will be based on the item's share of the total order value. If multiple notes were purchased in a single transaction, StudentShop calculates and deducts only the proportion of the PayPal fee that applies to the refunded item, not the entire fee (PayPal typically charges ~2.9% plus a fixed $0.30 per domestic transaction made in AUD). For example, if you purchase two notes together for $10 and $30, and refund the $10 note, only 25% of the PayPal fee (~$0.37) from that transaction will be deducted from your refund.
