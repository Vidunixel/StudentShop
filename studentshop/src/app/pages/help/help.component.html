Help

How it works

StudentShop is an online marketplace for Australian high school study notes. You can either upload your own PDF notes for subjects like VCE, HSC, WACE, QCE, SACE or TCE to earn money, or browse and buy notes created by other students. We cover all major curriculum levels, so you can find notes for the same subjects you study in Year 11 and 12.

  • As a seller: Upload your PDF notes, set your price, and StudentShop will automatically create preview pages so others can sample your work before buying.

  • As a buyer: Browse or search by subject to find notes to purchase or access for free. You’ll see reviews and sample pages to help you decide before buying.



For support, email us at support@studentshop.com.au.

Selling
How do I sell my notes?

Log in or create an account, then click Sell. Enter a title and brief description, upload your PDF notes, select the subject, and set a price. StudentShop will review your upload for quality and compliance before it becomes publicly listed.

What fees does StudentShop take?

StudentShop takes a 15% commission on what remains after GST and the item’s share of the PayPal transaction fee have been deducted. For each sale, we apply deductions in this order: GST is removed first; the item’s portion of the PayPal fee is taken next; then StudentShop’s 15% commission is calculated from the balance remaining after those deductions.

Why is my sale pending?

When someone buys your notes, the sale is first marked as pending. This is because it can take up to two days for the payment to clear and be credited to your StudentShop account. Once the payment is confirmed, the status will change from pending to completed, and the funds will be available in your account balance.

Why is my sale rejected?

A sale might be marked as rejected if there’s an issue. This can happen if the buyer requested a refund or if we found a problem (like a terms-of-service violation) with the notes. Remember, StudentShop reviews every uploaded note for compliance. If a note breaks our rules or a buyer is not satisfied, we may cancel the sale. In these cases, the sale will not go through, and you won’t receive payment for that transaction.

Buying
Do I need to pay for free notes?

No. Free notes can be claimed without entering any payment details. You do need a StudentShop account, but free notes cost $0. You can access them at no charge as soon as you claim them.

Can I download notes?

If a note is marked Downloadable , you can download it as a PDF file to your device at anytime. Notes marked as Downloadable  are only available to view in your Collection while you’re online.

For details about refunds, please see our full Refund Policy.

Withdrawals
Why is my withdrawal pending?

After you request a withdrawal, the transfer can take a few days. We may take up to 5 days to review and approve the withdrawal request. During this time, the status will show as pending. Once approved, you’ll receive the funds in your PayPal account. The exact timing may also depend on PayPal’s processing times.
