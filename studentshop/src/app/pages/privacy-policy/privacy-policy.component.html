Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/11/2025
1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from users to operate StudentShop:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email, school, and selected subjects.

  • Payment Information: To process sales, we collect PayPal identifiers (email or phone) only when you make a withdrawal request. We do not store your full financial or credit card details. All payments go through PayPal.

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect data about your use of the Service, such as logins, pages viewed, and IP addresses. We may also use cookies and similar technologies to enhance security and user experience (e.g. keeping you logged in).

  • User Content: Any notes, reviews, or user profile information you provide are stored on our servers.

  • Support Communications: If you contact us (via email or in-app), we may store your communications for support and record-keeping.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and Improve Services: Create your account, display your uploaded notes for sale, and enable the marketplace functionality. Improve our AI moderation and app features.

  • Process Payments: Transmit necessary information (e.g. PayPal Identifier) to PayPal to send your earnings when you withdraw, and to process buyers’ payments.

  • Communicate: Send service-related emails (e.g. account confirmations, receipts, updates, or responses to inquiries). We may also send you optional notifications or newsletters if you opt in.

  • Security and Fraud Prevention: Verify accounts, prevent fraudulent transactions, and secure the Service (for example, by detecting unusual activity and blocking attacks).

  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations (e.g. tax reporting) and our policies (e.g. reviewing reported content).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data as follows:

  • With PayPal: We transmit your PayPal identifier and related transaction details to PayPal to process withdrawals and payments. You should review PayPal’s own privacy policy.

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors (e.g. analytics providers, email service, cloud hosting) who assist us. These parties are obligated to protect your data and use it only for contracted purposes.

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to respond to valid legal requests (court orders, subpoenas, government requests). We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

  • Protection of Rights: To protect StudentShop or others (for example, if we believe your actions are unlawful or violate our policies).

  • With Consent: If you allow it, e.g. if you contact us and provide additional information.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures such as encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest, regular security assessments, and access controls. However, no internet service is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee that unauthorised third parties will never bypass our protections.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. For example, we keep account information while your account exists and transaction records for as long as required by law (e.g. tax regulations). You may close your account at any time; upon closure we will delete or de-identify most of your data, except where we need to keep records (e.g. for legal claims or compliance).

6. Children’s Privacy

If you are under 18, you should use StudentShop only with a parent or guardian’s permission. We do not knowingly collect more personal information from minors than is necessary. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected personal data of a child without consent, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as:

  • Access & Correction: You can view or edit most account information (name, email, school, subjects) in your account settings. You may request copies of your data or corrections.

  • Deletion: You may delete your account by contacting us. We will delete your personal data subject to any legal obligations to retain certain data. (Note: deleting your account does not delete notes or reviews you have posted, but it will remove your profile.)

  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can unsubscribe from newsletters or marketing messages. Service-related communications (like purchase receipts) cannot be opted out of.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality (session management) and analytics. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect how the app works. We may use Google Analytics or other services to understand usage trends; these collect anonymised usage data. Users can opt-out of analytics tracking via their browser if desired.

9. Compliance with Laws

StudentShop complies with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) for handling personal data. We also adhere to applicable consumer protection and data security laws.

10. Third-Party Services

Our Service links to or integrates third-party services (e.g. PayPal). Those services have their own privacy policies. StudentShop is not responsible for third parties’ privacy practices. By using our Service, you also agree to any terms of those services.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” will indicate the last change. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

For questions about privacy or to make requests regarding your personal data, please contact us at support@studentshop.com.au. We will respond to reasonable requests in accordance with applicable law.
